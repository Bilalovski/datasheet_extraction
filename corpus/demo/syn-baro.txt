SYN-P90 Barometric Pressure Sensor


The SYN-P90 is a digital barometric pressure sensor for altimetry.

  Interface .................... I2C and SPI
  Supply voltage ............... 1.7 V to 3.6 V
  Operating temperature ........ -40 to +85 degC
  Pressure range ............... 260 hPa to 1260 hPa
  Absolute accuracy ............ +/- 0.6 hPa
