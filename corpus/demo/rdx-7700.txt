ACME Microwave Systems
RDX-7700 Automotive Radar Transceiver
Preliminary Datasheet, Rev. B


1. Overview

The RDX-7700 is a single-chip FMCW radar transceiver intended for automotive
forward-looking applications such as adaptive cruise control and automatic
emergency braking.


2. Electrical Characteristics

  Parameter                    Min      Typ      Max      Unit
  --------------------------------------------------------------
  Supply voltage               3.0      3.3      3.6      V
  Power consumption            --       2.4      3.1      W
  Operating temperature       -40       --       125      degC


3. RF Characteristics

  Operating band                        76 - 81 GHz
  Sweep bandwidth                       4000 MHz
  Transmit channels                     3
  Receive channels                      4


4. Detection Performance

  Maximum range                         250 m
  Range resolution                      0.04 m
  Azimuth field of view                 +/-60 deg
  Elevation field of view               +/-15 deg


5. Interface

Raw ADC data is streamed off-chip over LVDS.
