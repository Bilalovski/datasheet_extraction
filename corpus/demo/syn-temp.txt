Nordic Sensing
SYN-T20 Digital Temperature Sensor


The SYN-T20 is a high-accuracy digital temperature sensor.

  Interface .................... I2C
  Supply voltage ............... 1.8 V to 3.6 V
  Operating temperature range .. -40 degC to 125 degC
  Accuracy ..................... +/-0.25 degC (maximum) over the full range
