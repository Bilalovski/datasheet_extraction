LS-16 Rotating Lidar Scanner
Helios Photonics


The LS-16 is a rotating lidar scanner for mobile mapping and mid-range
obstacle detection.


  Maximum range ................ 100 m
  Vertical field of view ....... 30 deg
  Horizontal field of view ..... 360 deg
  Interface .................... Ethernet
  Power ........................ 8 W typical
